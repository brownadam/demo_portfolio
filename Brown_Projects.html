Scratch Game/Story, CSE

September 2015

Project Objective: To develop a game with user interaction that significantly alters events, as well as a clear objective with multiple stages.

My partner and I documented our work through Scratch comments and a detailed Project Design Logbook with our initial brainstorm and early drafts and versions of the game. This project introduced me to the engineering design process. The drag-and-drop format of MIT's Scratch programming language eased me into the world of algorithms and practical applications. We encountered challenges with transitional elements and sprite movement, but we persevered through our difficulties. I took up a central role devising alternative solutions and utilized many online resources to guide me through the process. Our decisions were made unanimously, with each of us providing input on level design and graphics.
